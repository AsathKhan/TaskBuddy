Support

Welcome to the TaskBuddy Support page! We are here to help you with any issues you might have.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I reset my password? You can reset your password by clicking on 'Forgot Password' at the login screen.
  • How can I contact support? You can contact our support team via email at support@taskbuddy.com.
  • Where can I find the user guide? The user guide can be found in the help section of the dashboard.
  • What do I do if I encounter an error? Please take a screenshot of the error and send it to our support email for assistance.
  • Can I change my username? Yes, you can change your username in the profile settings of your dashboard.
  • How do I delete my account? If you wish to delete your account, please contact our support team for further assistance.
  • Are there any tutorials available? Yes, we have a range of tutorials available in the help section of your dashboard.
  • What should I do if I forget my username? If you've forgotten your username, you can recover it by clicking the 'Forgot Username' link on the login page.
  • Is there a mobile app available? Currently, we only have a web application, but we are working on a mobile app for future release.
